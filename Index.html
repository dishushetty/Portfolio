My Projects

IR Thermometer

The IR Thermometer project is a cutting-edge application of thermal sensing technology combined with Arduino microcontroller capabilities designed to accurately measure temperature using infrared radiation.

By harnessing the principles of non-contact temperature measurement, this project provides a user-friendly and efficient method for monitoring temperature levels in various scenarios.
